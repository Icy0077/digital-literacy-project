# ✅ Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist

## 🛡️ General Online Safety Tips

1. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts and change them regularly.  
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra security.  
3. Do not click suspicious links received through unknown emails, SMS, or social media messages.  
4. Verify website URLs before entering login or personal information.  
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.  
6. Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.

---

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety Tips

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, including people claiming to be bank officials.  
2. Remember: entering a UPI PIN means you are **paying money**, not receiving it.  
3. Always check payment requests carefully before approving collect requests in UPI apps.  
4. Use only official banking or payment apps downloaded from trusted app stores.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime in India

1. Report online fraud at the **National Cyber Crime Portal**:  
   👉 https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  

2. Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline**: **1930** (available 24×7)
