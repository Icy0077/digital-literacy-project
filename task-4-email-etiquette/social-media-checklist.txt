✅ Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✔️ Do’s
--1.Use respectful language while posting or commenting online.
--2.Check facts before sharing information to avoid spreading misinformation.
--3.Maintain privacy settings to protect personal information.
--4.Think before posting, as online content creates a permanent digital footprint.
--5.Use social media for learning and networking, such as following educational pages and professionals.

❌ Don’ts
--1.Do not share personal details like address, phone number, or passwords publicly.
--2.Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
--3.Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
--4.Avoid clicking suspicious links or unknown messages.
--5.Do not post misleading or unverified news.
